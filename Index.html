Things to do in Madrid!

Madrid is known for its fusion of historical and modern culture, as well as its community. Madrilenians are known for being the best to go out and have the best cooks. What a perfect mix, right? Its extensive list of different types of gastronomy, nightlife, culture and museums will complete a trip to remember. On this website you will be able to discover the best places to go.

In addition to the activities mentioned on this page, in Madrid there is also a large dimension of options when it comes to buying clothes. Gran Vía is known for being a luxury shopping street that offers most of the most luxurious and well-known clothing brands.

The thing that makes this city so magical is the ease to travel around it. Public transportation is the key to this. A single journey on any kind of public transportation in Madrid starts at €1.50. The flat rate for bus tickets, but multi-zone single journeys can cost up to €2 on the metro and €5.50 on the commuter train (cercanías). Only use Cercanias for longer distances and tedious transfers among trains. All transportation methods have an additional charge for going to and from the airport. In case that you are relying a lot on public transport, consider a 10-trip ticket pack. This card costs €3 and the 10-trip ticket pack €12.20, which allows it to be shared among family or friends. Also, you can recharge as many times as you want!
